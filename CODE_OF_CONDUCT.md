# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a respectful, welcoming, and constructive space for everyone.

Contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to engage in ways that support:

- respectful communication
- professional collaboration
- curiosity without hostility
- inclusive participation

## Expected behavior

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- giving helpful and specific feedback
- assuming good intent while staying honest
- being respectful of different experience levels
- focusing critique on ideas, code, and decisions rather than people
- helping keep discussions practical and solution-oriented

## Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or personal attacks
- insulting, demeaning, or discriminatory language
- deliberate intimidation
- bad-faith disruption of discussions, issues, or reviews
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, or other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
